# 📊 Electronics Product Sales Dashboard | Looker Studio (Google Data Studio)

Excited to share one of my **Data Analytics** projects! 🚀

I designed and developed an **interactive Electronics Product Sales Dashboard** using **Looker Studio (Google Data Studio)** to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights. The dashboard enables users to monitor sales performance, analyze customer purchasing trends, and make informed business decisions through interactive visualizations.

## 🎯 Dashboard Highlights

📈 **Executive KPIs**

* 💰 **Total Sales:** **60.5K**
* 📦 **Total Quantity Ordered:** **400 Units**
* 📅 Interactive **Date Range Filter** for dynamic analysis

📊 **Sales Performance Analysis**

* Weekly Sales Trend to monitor revenue performance over time.
* City-wise Sales & Quantity Ordered comparison to identify regional sales trends.

🗺️ **Geographical Insights**

* Interactive map visualization showing sales distribution across different cities.
* Easy identification of high-performing locations and market opportunities.

🛍️ **Product Performance**

* Product-wise Sales donut chart to understand revenue contribution by each product.
* Product-wise Quantity Ordered table to identify best-selling products.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

✅ Identified peak sales weeks through trend analysis.

✅ Compared sales performance across multiple cities.

✅ Highlighted top-selling products based on quantity ordered.

✅ Created an interactive dashboard for quick and data-driven business decisions.
